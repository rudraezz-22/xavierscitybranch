Welcome to St. Xavier School

Dear Parents and Students, it gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to St. Xavier School, where we are committed to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders.

Our institution stands as a beacon of quality education in Ballia, combining academic excellence with character building. We believe that every child is unique and possesses immense potential waiting to be discovered and developed.

Through our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-centric approach, we strive to create an environment where learning is joyful, creativity is encouraged, and values are instilled. Join us in this transformative journey of education.

Our Academic Programs

Primary School

Classes I-V focus on foundational learning with activity-based pedagogy, fostering curiosity and building strong basics in all subjects.

Middle School

Classes VI-VIII emphasize comprehensive development with introduction to advanced concepts, co-curricular activities, and skill enhancement.

Senior School

Classes IX-X prepare students for board exams and competitive entrance tests with specialized faculty and comprehensive study programs.
